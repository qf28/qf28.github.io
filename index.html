I am an Assistant Professor in the School of Computer Science at Nanjing University of Science and Technology. My research focus on Software Architecture and Software Evolution.

I received my Ph.D degree in Computer Science from Drexel University in 2019, advised by Dr. Yuanfang Cai. I received my B.S. degree from Renming University of China in 2009.
